Utility Functions for Structured Arrays
=======================================

This module provides a collection of utilities to manipulate structured arrays,
including functions for appending fields, dropping fields, renaming fields,
joining arrays, and more. Most of these functions were initially implemented
by John Hunter for matplotlib and have been rewritten and extended for
convenience.
