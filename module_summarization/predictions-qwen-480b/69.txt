===================
Isotonic Regression
===================

.. currentmodule:: sklearn.isotonic

The :mod:`sklearn.isotonic` module implements isotonic regression, a technique for fitting a free-form line to a sequence of observations such that the fitted line is non-decreasing (or non-increasing) everywhere, and lies as close to the observations as possible.

Isotonic regression is useful for fitting monotonic relationships between variables, especially when there is no assumption about the functional form of the relationship.
