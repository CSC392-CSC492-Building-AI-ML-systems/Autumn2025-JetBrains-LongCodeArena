w_bins
======

The ``w_bins`` command is used to display information and statistics about binning in a WEST simulation, or to modify the binning for the current iteration of a WEST simulation.

Usage
-----

.. code-block:: bash

    w_bins info [-n N_ITER] [--detail]
    w_bins rebin [--confirm] [--detail] [-n N_ITER]

Commands
--------

info
~~~~

Display information about binning.

Options:
- ``-n, --n-iter``: Consider initial points of segment N_ITER (default: current iteration).
- ``--detail``: Display detailed per-bin information in addition to summary information.

rebin
~~~~~

Rebuild current iteration with new binning.

Options:
- ``--confirm``: Commit the revised iteration to HDF5; without this option, the effects of the new binning are only calculated and printed.
- ``--detail``: Display detailed per-bin information in addition to summary information.
- ``-n, --n-iter``: Consider initial points of segment N_ITER (default: current iteration).
